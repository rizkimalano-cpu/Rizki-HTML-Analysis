Property Card Enhancement — Android A/B Test Results

Interim read — monitoring continues
Test: Property Card Enhancement (MD-2282 follow-up, Android)
Period: June 12–21, 2026 (10 days, both variants)
Platform: Android  |  Categories: Property for Rent, Property for Sale

Visual Comparison

What changed: Enhanced card increases image size, restructures info hierarchy with price → title → location → specs in a clearer vertical flow, and improves separation between cards. Hypothesis: easier to read and scan, leading to higher click-through.

TL;DR

  • Primary metric — Adlist to adview % (volume): significantly positive in both categories — Rent +4.66% (p<0.0001), Sale +1.84% (p<0.0001).
  • % unique user going to adview (user metric): flat in both categories. Holds.
  • Adviews per user: significantly positive in both categories.

Enhanced card is achieving its hypothesis of making property info easier to scan and click. Continuing to monitor for full picture including downstream conversion.
