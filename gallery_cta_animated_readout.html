01Problem & hypothesis

Why we built this.

The problem

On Cars for Sale listing pages, roughly 90% of buyers open the image gallery — it’s the most-visited part of the page. But the only way to contact the seller is on the adview page itself. A buyer who’s ready to act while browsing photos has to close the gallery first, creating friction at the exact moment their interest is highest.

Our hypothesis

If we put a contact CTA directly inside the gallery — mirroring whatever options already exist on the adview page — we remove that friction and convert more browsers into responders, without changing how people actually browse images. Success looks like: higher Browser→Responder conversion and more leads, with gallery open rate holding steady.

02The change

Same gallery. One new button.

Control shows the standard photo gallery — no way to contact the seller without exiting back to the adview page. Treatment adds the car’s title, price, and a pinned Chat / Call / WhatsApp bar right inside the gallery overlay, mirroring whatever contact options already exist on the adview.

04Incrementality

Are these new leads, or moved leads?

The gallery CTA is a new place to submit a lead, so its volume isn’t automatically new demand — some of it is users who would have messaged the seller anyway, just through a more convenient button. We split each platform’s CTA leads into net-new vs. shifted, using the control arm’s natural lead rate as the counterfactual. Click a row to see the split.
